Our Story Our Story

From a backyard poultry farm discovery to building replicable AI-powered climate-focused circular economies across Africa.

Our breakthrough began with a startling realization at Select Chicken Farm Uganda Limited. In analyzing their production cycle, we discovered they were consuming over 600 tons of maize annually—generating approximately 180 tons of agricultural waste that was being catastrophically mismanaged. This waste was either burned, releasing harmful CO₂ emissions, or left to decompose naturally, a process taking 6-12 months while attracting pests and creating health hazards.

This revelation sparked a critical insight: if one poultry operation could generate such massive waste streams, the problem was exponentially larger across Uganda's agricultural sector. We estimated that similar facilities nationwide were burning millions of dollars worth of potential resources while simultaneously importing expensive feeds and fertilizers.

The Solution The Solution

The Blueprint: A Replicable Circular Economy Powered by AI

This challenge became our opportunity. We developed a scalable blueprint for circular economy transformation that extends far beyond poultry to encompass all agricultural enterprises. The genius lies in the replicability: whether it's maize waste from poultry operations, rice husks from mills, or crop residues from farms, our AI-optimized BSF processing can transform any organic waste stream into profitable resources. What started as solving one farm's waste problem has become a model for agricultural transformation across East Africa proving that environmental solutions and economic prosperity can work hand in hand..

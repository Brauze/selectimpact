The Enterprise Challenge

Select Chicken Farm Uganda Limited identified a critical inefficiency in their operations that was costing millions and creating significant environmental liability.

Critical Discovery

Operational analysis revealed that the facility was consuming over 600 tons of maize annually, generating approximately 180 tons of agricultural waste that was being systematically mismanaged.

The cost implications:

  • Direct disposal costs
  • Environmental compliance risks
  • Lost revenue opportunities
  • Health and safety liabilities

Market Opportunity Analysis

If a single poultry operation generated such substantial waste streams, the market opportunity across Uganda's agricultural sector represented a multimillion-dollar transformation potential.
